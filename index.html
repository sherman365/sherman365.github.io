The Bull Pen is in the farmyard at the edge of the wood, overlooking the pond. It is recently converted, upcycling wherever possible.

There is an open plan living area, a twin bedroom and a double room with views over the pond. There is a log burner for winter evenings.

Situated on a working farm, our retreat offers a harmonious blend of tranquility and rural activity. You may encounter a gentle flow of walkers, animals, and occasional farm vehicles passing by. It's essential to note that there could also be intermittent noise from the workshop/farm during the day, and the crowing of cockerels may greet you early in the morning.

About

The farm includes award-winning ancient woodland run as a nature reserve - great for a quiet weekend away, or a longer rural retreat. Highlights include butterflies, bluebells and nightingales in season. You are welcome to walk in the woodland on the farm.
